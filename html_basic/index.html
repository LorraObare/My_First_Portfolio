Hello everyone, welcome to my first portfolio

I have doing my front end program with Alx Africa and so far I really love the process.

Why do I do it?

One thing for sure, Alx has taught me the importance of sacrifice, doing this program has made me to get up early in the morning so that I can set the right time to do my modules.

Also, I have learnt that consistency is the key to success, I have to be consistent in doing my modules and also in learning new things.

It was so hard to find the balance at the start but I genuinely love what I do and that is why I am still here.

Finally, I do it because I want to be a better version of myself and I know that this is the right path to get there.

What are my goals?

My goals are to be a full stack developer, I want to be able to create beautiful and functional websites and applications.

I also want to be able to work with a team of developers and learn from them.

One goal that is rooted deeply in my heart is to teach street children tech. Tech has really empowered me and made me so confident and happy, I cant imagine what that would do to someone with the same passion given the same opportunity.

To Alx, I am truly grateful
